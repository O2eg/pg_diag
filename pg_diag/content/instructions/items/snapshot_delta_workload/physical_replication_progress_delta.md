# Physical Replication Progress Delta

This instruction belongs to report item `snapshot_delta_workload.physical_replication_progress_delta`.

## What this item shows
- WAL generated, sent, written, flushed, and replayed for each physical replication sender during the window.
- Sender and replay throughput derived from LSN movement.

## What to watch
- Generated bytes growing faster than replayed bytes and senders with little or no replay progress.

## Automatic evaluation
- No severity is assigned because asynchronous and intentionally delayed standbys can legitimately lag.

## Interval coverage
- A row requires the same sender PID and backend start time at both endpoints.
- Sender restarts, disconnects, promotion, or missing LSNs make the interval unavailable rather than zero.

## Common fault causes
- Network throughput, receiver or replay stalls, storage latency, recovery conflicts, and intentional replay delay.
